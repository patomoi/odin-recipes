Empanadas

Description

Empanadas have been a staple in South America household for years. This is a recipe for Chilean empanadas. This recipe makes about a dozen large empanadas, depending on the size.

The empanadas are a dish made of bread and filled with meat or cheese or various combinations of different foods.

Ingredients

Filling

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1 cup raisins
  • 1 cup chopped black olives
  • 3 large hard-cooked eggs, chopped
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
